## What it is
Go-based TUI by the Charm team. Multi-provider, LSP + MCP, AGENTS.md auto-initialisation. Multi-platform including FreeBSD.

**Lineage note:** Independent codebase from `sst/opencode`. Sometimes referred to informally as the Go-lineage successor in the TUI niche; this is descriptive of market positioning, not a fork relationship.
